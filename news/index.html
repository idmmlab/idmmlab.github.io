Apr 29, 2026

Our paper “Modeling the hierarchical microstructure of L-PBF Ti-6Al-4V: a multiphysics framework for texture-induced mechanical anisotropy” has been accepted for publication in Virtual and Physical Prototyping (IF 8.8, JCR Top 10%, Engineering & Manufacturing).

The work develops a multiphysics framework that links laser powder bed fusion (L-PBF) process conditions to the resulting hierarchical microstructure and texture-induced mechanical anisotropy in Ti-6Al-4V, bridging process physics, microstructure evolution, and component-level mechanical response.

Virtual and Physical Prototyping (IF 8.8, 공학·제조 분야 JCR 상위 10%) SCI 저널에 논문이 게재 승인되었습니다. 레이저 분말 베드 융합(L-PBF) 공정으로 제조된 Ti-6Al-4V 합금의 계층적 미세구조와 그에 따른 기계적 이방성을 다물리 모델링 프레임워크로 해석한 연구로, 공정 물리·미세구조 변화·부품 수준 기계적 거동을 통합적으로 연결합니다.
Apr 24, 2026

The Intelligent Design for Materials & Manufacturing (IDMM) Lab has officially launched at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Soongsil University. The lab pursues research at the intersection of generative AI, multiphysics modeling, and digital engineering for advanced manufacturing — with application focus on metal additive manufacturing, microstructure design across diverse material systems, and next-generation aerospace component design.

We are actively recruiting graduate students (M.S. and Ph.D.) and undergraduate research interns. Recruiting topics span Physics-guided generative AI for composite materials, materials design foundation models, multiscale inverse design, additive manufacturing simulation, and Agentic AI for aerospace and mobility component CAD generation. See the Join Us page for details.

숭실대학교 기계공학부 지능형 소재 및 제조 설계 연구실 (IDMM Lab) 이 정식 출범했습니다. 생성형 AI, 멀티피직스·멀티스케일 모델링, 디지털 엔지니어링을 결합하여 첨단 제조 분야의 새로운 설계 방법론을 연구합니다. 박사후연구원, 석·박사 과정생, 학부 인턴을 모집하고 있습니다 — 자세한 모집 분야는 Join Us 페이지를 참고해 주세요.
Mar 01, 2026

Prof. Kang-Hyun Lee joined the School of Mechanical Engineering at Soongsil University as Assistant Professor on March 1, 2026.

Before joining Soongsil University, he was a Postdoctoral Associate in the DeCoDE Lab at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), where he contributed to a DARPA-funded research project on developing generative AI for the design and manufacturing of reusable rocket components. He received his Ph.D. and M.S. from the Department of Aerospace Engineering at Seoul National University, with a research focus on multiphysics and multiscale simulation of additive manufacturing processes.

이강현 교수가 2026년 3월 1일자로 숭실대학교 기계공학부에 조교수로 부임했습니다. 부임 전 미국 MIT 기계공학과 DeCoDE Lab 박사후연구원으로 재직하며 DARPA 지원의 재사용 로켓 부품 생성형 설계·제조 연구에 참여했습니다. 서울대학교 항공우주공학과에서 적층제조 공정의 멀티피직스·멀티스케일 시뮬레이션을 주제로 박사 및 석사 학위를 취득했습니다.
