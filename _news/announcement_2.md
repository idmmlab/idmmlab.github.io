**Prof. Kang-Hyun Lee** joined the School of Mechanical Engineering at
Soongsil University as Assistant Professor on March 1, 2026.

Before joining Soongsil University, he was a Postdoctoral Associate in
the [DeCoDE Lab](https://decode.mit.edu/) at the Department of
Mechanical Engineering, Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT),
where he contributed to a DARPA-funded research project on developing
generative AI for the design and manufacturing of reusable rocket
components. He received his Ph.D. and M.S. from the Department of
Aerospace Engineering at Seoul National University, with a research
focus on multiphysics and multiscale simulation of additive
manufacturing processes.

**이강현 교수**가 2026년 3월 1일자로 숭실대학교 기계공학부에 조교수로
부임했습니다. 부임 전 미국 MIT 기계공학과 DeCoDE Lab 박사후연구원으로
재직하며 DARPA 지원의 재사용 로켓 부품 생성형 설계·제조 연구에 참여했습니다.
서울대학교 항공우주공학과에서 적층제조 공정의 멀티피직스·멀티스케일
시뮬레이션을 주제로 박사 및 석사 학위를 취득했습니다.
