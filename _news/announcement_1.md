The **Intelligent Design for Materials & Manufacturing (IDMM) Lab** has
officially launched at the School of Mechanical Engineering, Soongsil
University. The lab pursues research at the intersection of generative
AI, multiphysics modeling, and digital engineering for advanced
manufacturing — with application focus on metal additive manufacturing,
microstructure design across diverse material systems, and
next-generation aerospace component design.

We are actively recruiting **graduate students (M.S. and Ph.D.)** and
**undergraduate research interns**. Recruiting topics span Physics-guided
generative AI for composite materials, materials design foundation
models, multiscale inverse design, additive manufacturing simulation,
and Agentic AI for aerospace and mobility component CAD generation. See
the [Join Us]({{ '/join/' | relative_url }}) page for details.

정식 출범했습니다. 생성형 AI, 멀티피직스·멀티스케일 모델링, 디지털 엔지니어링을
결합하여 첨단 제조 분야의 새로운 설계 방법론을 연구합니다.
